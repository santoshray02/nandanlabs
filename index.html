VLSI Guru

Durgesh Nandan

Graduated in electronics & communicationfrom Rajeev Gandhi Technical University, Madhya Pradesh, India in 2009 and the M.Tech in Microelectronics & VLSI Design from Rajeev Gandhi Technical University, Madhya Pradesh, India in 2013.currently working towards the Ph.D at Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Jaypee University of Engineering and Technology, Guna, Madhya Pradesh, India.
He served as a assistant professor and Head of department in the Department of Electronics and communication Engineering, IASSCOM fortune institutes of technology (IFIT), India, from March 2013 to July 2014. He is the author or a co-author of more than 10 papers in peer reviewed international journals and conference proceedings. His Research interests include computer arithmetic, low power logic synthesis and digital signal processing.
